By accessing or using CaseWise, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

Cookie Policy

CaseWise uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and understand how our Service is used. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and track your activity.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting Service functionality.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as language settings, login information, and customization options. They enable us to provide a personalized experience tailored to your needs.

Analytical Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our Service by collecting and reporting usage statistics. This information helps us improve our features and user experience.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your activity across websites to deliver targeted advertisements and marketing content. They help us understand which promotions are most effective.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies, or be notified when a cookie is being sent. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to use portions of our Service. You can also opt out of third-party cookies through industry-standard opt-out mechanisms.

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage to collect information about your browsing activities and interactions with our Service.

Data Security

CaseWise implements comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure storage of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection practices
  • Incident response procedures
  • Compliance with industry security standards

While we strive to protect your information using reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use our Service at your own risk.

How We Share Your Information

CaseWise does not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Service, including hosting providers, payment processors, analytics services, and customer support platforms. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, such as in response to subpoenas, court orders, or government requests. We will provide notice of such requests when legally permitted.

Business Transfers

If CaseWise is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your information.

Aggregated Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized information that cannot identify you personally with third parties for research, marketing, analytics, and other purposes.

User Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you provide explicit consent to do so.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your information in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications and certain data collection practices.
  • Restriction: You may request that we limit how we use your information.
  • Objection: You can object to certain processing of your information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below.

Children's Privacy

CaseWise is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users between 13 and 18 years old, we provide additional protections and limit the collection and use of information as required by applicable laws. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to CaseWise should contact us immediately.
